CAR RESTORATION

  • Resource & Time Management

Beyond sports, I have a passion for anything with an engine and four wheels. Fortunately, I have the opportunity to restore cars with my dad. In 2022, I completed the restoration of my dream car, a VW Golf Mk2. It took two years of daily dedication to bring the car to impeccable condition, adhering to the principle that 'You either do it right, or do it twice.' This project was both challenging and immensely rewarding. I gained extensive knowledge, not only in technical skills but also in soft skills such as time and resource management.
